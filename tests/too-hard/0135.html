Property Description for 6710 Hillside Ln Wauwatosa WI 53213-3257

6710 Hillside Ln Wauwatosa WI 53213-3257 - Stop looking and start packing! Quaint 2BR home with 2.5 car garage in the Heart of Wauwatosa! Interior features large front living room. Kitchen with appliances included. Lower level offers a finished rec room, toilet, workshop area, and plenty of storage space. Long driveway to private back yard. Additional storage shed. Exceptional location backing to Honey Creek Pkwy. and Jacobus Park with plenty of nature, walking, and biking trails. Quiet no-thru street. A short walk to great restaurants!
