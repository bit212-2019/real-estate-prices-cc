  • Description
Experience the energy of 33rd - only steps away from a huge park with outdoor pool - with this new Marda Loop infill, built with pride by luxury and SAM award winning builder: Cornerstone Homes. This home was built with style and functionality in mind. The open floor plan on the main level is enhanced by 9’ ceilings, an energy efficient fireplace and extensive natural light provided by oversized windows. Engineered hardwood flooring spans the main floor with a galley kitchen design as the center of the layout. S/S appliance package and an oversized island with gorgeous quartz countertops is sure to impress. The mudroom showcases elegant tile flooring with masterfully handcrafted lockers. The upper floor includes three bedrooms all with custom built WIC’s, an upper laundry and a four piece bathroom. The large and functional master bedroom includes ample storage space in the custom built closet, as well as a spa style ensuite with skylight, dual sinks, free standing soaker tub and desirable full tile
