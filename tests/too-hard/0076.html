2885 Possum Trot Rd. Billings MO 65610 Beautiful rustic contemporary on 19.93 acres (M/L). This all brick ranch with finished walkout basement offers an open kitchen, great room with a wall of windows to enjoy the views. Two large covered decks with vaulted roof line are great for outdoor entertaining and living space. Lower level has recently been finished with recreation room, bedroom and bath plus potential office or 4th bedroom. Fireplace upstairs is ducted for heating plus a wood stove on the lower level keeps utility bills very low. Over sized garage. Truly a great property. This home is located less than 5 minutes to James River for floating and camping. Square footage is approximate per Stone County tax records, buyer to do their own due diligence for the square footage.
