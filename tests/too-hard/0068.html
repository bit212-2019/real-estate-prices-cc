Highly upgraded & air conditioned corner lot bungalow that backs to walking paths & green space. 10 ft main floor ceilings & 9 ft in the basement! A front flex room features hardwood flooring & a bay window. The kitchen has extended height white units, granite counters & LG stainless steel appliances. The bright dining nook has huge double aspect windows. The living room features surround sound wiring & a gas fireplace with adjacent windows. The master retreat has an upgraded walk in closet & a 5 pc en-suite. A second bedroom, a 4 pc bathroom & a laundry/mud room complete the main level. Downstairs has in floor heat, massive recreation & family room areas, a future wet bar area, a huge third bedroom with a walk in closet, a 4 pc bathroom, a hobby room (or den) & masses of storage in the utility room. The rear yard offers a huge deck with privacy panels, a screen of trees, a lawn area & walking path access! New shingles & downspouts too! Ample nearby parking for guests! COME VIEW THE 3D TOUR NOW!
